     2 HOUR CEILIDH  |  420

    Our 2 hour ceilidh consists of two sets of 45 minutes with a 30 minute break in between. In the past we've found that this works best if you have organised another form of entertainment for the event as well.

    3 HOUR STANDARD CEILIDH  |  540

    By far our most popular arrangement, our standard ceilidh consists of two sets of 1 hour and 15 minutes with a 30 minute break in between. We find that this is the best option as we can get through plenty of dances with suitable breaks as well.

    EXTENDED CEILIDH  |  540+

    If you're looking for a longer ceilidh, we are happy to play extended sets around your requirements. The price is based upon our Standard Ceilidh, with 50 per extra hour of music or dancing.
